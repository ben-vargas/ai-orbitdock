Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

Self-hosted, no data collection

OrbitDock is entirely self-hosted. You run the server on your own hardware and all data, including conversations, code diffs, and configuration, stays in a SQLite database on your machine. We never see, access, or collect any of it. There is no analytics, telemetry, or tracking of any kind.

Third-party AI providers

OrbitDock connects to AI providers using your own API keys or CLI installations. Communication with those providers is governed by their privacy policies:

  • Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI Privacy Policy

TestFlight

If you install OrbitDock via Apple TestFlight, Apple may collect crash logs as described in Apple's Privacy Policy. This is standard TestFlight behavior and is not controlled by OrbitDock.
