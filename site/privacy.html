Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

Overview

OrbitDock is a self-hosted application. Your data stays on your own infrastructure. We do not operate servers that collect, store, or process your data. This privacy policy explains what data OrbitDock handles and how it works.

Data you control

OrbitDock consists of a server component that you run on your own hardware and client applications (macOS, iOS, web) that connect to your server. All data, including session logs, conversation content, code diffs, and configuration, is stored locally in a SQLite database on your server.

We never see, access, or collect this data.

Third-party AI providers

OrbitDock connects to AI providers (Anthropic for Claude Code, OpenAI for Codex) using your own API keys or CLI installations. Communication with these providers is governed by their respective privacy policies:

  • Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI Privacy Policy

OrbitDock does not proxy, intercept, or modify the content sent to or received from these providers beyond what is necessary for the application to function.

Network communication

The OrbitDock client apps communicate with your self-hosted server over HTTP and WebSocket connections. If you expose your server over the internet, you are responsible for securing the connection (e.g., using HTTPS via Cloudflare Tunnel or a reverse proxy).

Analytics and telemetry

OrbitDock does not include any analytics, telemetry, crash reporting, or tracking of any kind. No data is sent to us or any third party by OrbitDock itself.

App Store (TestFlight)

If you install OrbitDock via Apple TestFlight, Apple may collect crash logs and usage data as described in Apple's Privacy Policy. This is standard TestFlight behavior and is not controlled by OrbitDock.

Open source

OrbitDock is open source. You can audit the complete source code to verify these claims at github.com/Robdel12/OrbitDock.

Children's privacy

OrbitDock is a developer tool and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date. Since OrbitDock is self-hosted, changes to this policy do not affect data you have already stored on your own infrastructure.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on GitHub or reach out via the support page.
